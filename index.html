Important disclaimer

This is an educational estimator. Radiation dose varies by equipment, altitude, route, body size, scan protocol, and other factors. Do not use this as a medical device or regulatory dosimeter.

Why use this?

Are you tracking your radiation exposure? You should be.

Every year, millions of people undergo X-rays, CT scans, and even full-body DEXA scans—often without realizing how much radiation they’re accumulating over time.

The truth? Most patients don’t understand:

  • How much radiation each test exposes them to
  • What the safe limits are
  • When they might be going beyond what’s necessary

That’s where MyRadiationTracker comes in.

👉 Track your exposure
Log every scan—X-rays, CTs, DEXA, and more—and see your cumulative radiation in one place.

👉 Know your limits
Instantly understand whether you’re within safe exposure levels, based on global medical guidelines.

👉 Make informed decisions
Before your next scan, check your history and have smarter conversations with your doctor.

👉 Avoid unnecessary tests
Many people unknowingly repeat scans or opt for “routine” imaging that may not be needed.
